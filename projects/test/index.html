Overview

As a Design Lead at Portima, I spearheaded the renovation of Brio, a core product for insurance brokers in Belgium. This involved modernizing the platform with AI integration, cloud technologies, and establishing a long-term vision and roadmap to address technical debt and align with industry needs.

My Role

  • Visionary Leadership: Defined the roadmap and vision for Brio’s future.
  • Stakeholder Management: Collaborated with brokers, insurance companies, and internal teams to ensure alignment and support.
  • User Research: Observed broker workflows and conducted interviews to deeply understand client needs.
  • Agile Collaboration: Worked closely with technical teams to create feasible, impactful solutions.

Key Challenges

  1. Technical Debt: Addressing outdated systems while ensuring continued functionality for users.
  2. Stakeholder Alignment: Uniting diverse teams under a cohesive vision.
  3. Innovation Integration: Implementing AI and cloud solutions without disrupting existing workflows.

Innovations and Solutions

  • AI Integration:
    • Enabled automated communication analysis to prioritize tasks.
    • Designed next-step suggestions for brokers, streamlining workflows.
  • Unified Vision:
    • Fostered a shared understanding across teams, ensuring cohesive product development.
  • Scalable Infrastructure:
    • Introduced cloud technologies to enhance performance and future readiness.

Outcomes

  • Enhanced user satisfaction through intuitive AI-driven workflows.
  • Boosted team motivation and collaboration by aligning everyone with a clear, impactful vision.
  • Established a roadmap for sustainable innovation, preparing Brio for future industry needs.

Reflection

This project deepened my understanding of large-scale organizational challenges and legacy system modernization. It highlighted the importance of blending technical knowledge, user research, and design to drive meaningful change.

Feedback

“Your approach to aligning user needs with technical feasibility has been transformative. It truly feels like one cohesive system.” – Internal Stakeholder

Deliverables

  • Vision and roadmap for Brio.
  • Integrated AI workflows and cloud-based infrastructure plan.
  • Comprehensive user research and insights.

Tools and Techniques

  • Agile methodology.
  • User research and journey mapping.
  • Workshop facilitation.
  • Stakeholder interviews and co-creation sessions.
